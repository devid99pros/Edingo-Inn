Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your stay at Hotel Edingo Inn.

Where is Hotel Edingo Inn located? +

We're located at Sangitchowk‑24, Itahari, Sunsari, Nepal—just about 0.7 km from the town centre, and approximately 13 miles from Biratnagar Airport.

What are your check-in and check-out times? +

Check-in begins at 2:00 AM, with the latest arrival by 4:00 AM. Check-out is until 12:00 PM.

Is parking available onsite? +

Yes, we offer free private parking to all guests.

Do you have Wi-Fi? +

Absolutely! Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the property.

What dining options do you offer? +

Restaurant: On-site restaurant serving American cuisine, open for breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, high tea, and cocktails.

Bar & Cafe: Enjoy drinks, snacks, and light bites at the bar and adjoining cafe fully integrated with the restaurant facilities.

Breakfast: À la carte and American-style breakfasts are available each morning.

What types of rooms do you offer? +

We offer several accommodation options:

  • Deluxe Double/Twin Rooms
  • Superior Family Rooms
  • Suites, including Deluxe Suite options
Do rooms include private bathrooms and essential amenities? +

Yes—every room features private bathrooms, air-conditioning, flat-screen TV, desk, free toiletries, electric kettle, city views in select rooms, wardrobes, towels, and bed linens.

Are pets allowed? +

No—Hotel Edingo Inn does not allow pets, including dogs or cats.

Do you allow early check-in / late check-out? +

Early/late requests may be accommodated upon request, subject to availability. Standard times are 2:00 AM–4:00 AM for check-in, and check-out by 12 PM.

Is there an extra bed or cot available? +

Extra beds and cots may be available—please contact us directly when booking. Additional fees and confirmation apply.

Do you offer meeting or business facilities? +

Yes—we have meeting rooms and a business centre available, make sure to inquire when booking.

Any notable onsite facilities beyond rooms and dining? +
  • Terrace and outdoor seating area
  • Sun deck
  • Live music performances periodically
  • 24-hour front desk and security, including CCTV
  • Laundry service and car rental assistance
Is your hotel non-smoking? +

Yes—the property is smoke-free. A designated smoking area is available outdoors.
